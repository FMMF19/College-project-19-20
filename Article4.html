Detox Day

Before taking the memories of a legendary Tomorrowland experience back home, Global Journey invites you to an exclusive Detox Day on Monday after Tomorrowland. Enjoy the relaxing vibes of a green environment and let Global Journey spoil you.

Together with the People of Tomorrow, it's a unique place to share your experiences of a magnificent journey.

This event is not cashless. The Pearls on your Tomorrowland Bracelet cannot be used for food and drinks.

Enjoy your detox day!
